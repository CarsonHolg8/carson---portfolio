My Projects

Raytheon Stock Forecast Analysis

This project involves a comparative analysis of different stock forecasting methods for Raytheon Technologies. The study explores the effectiveness of Naïve, Moving Average, Linear Regression, and Exponential Smoothing methods, with a focus on determining which provides the lowest Root Mean Square Error (RMSE).

Naïve Method

This method assumes the next period's value will be the same as the current period's. Your analysis found this to be the most accurate method for this specific dataset.

**Key Finding:** Lowest RMSE

Moving Average

This forecast uses a 4-period moving average to smooth out short-term fluctuations in the stock data.

**RMSE:** 9.712

Linear Regression

A statistical approach that models the relationship between the stock's closing price and time to predict future values.

**RMSE:** 17.156

Exponential Smoothing

This method gives more weight to recent observations, providing a more responsive forecast to recent market trends.

**RMSE:** 5.529
